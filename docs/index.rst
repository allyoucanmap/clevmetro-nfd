Cleveland Metroparks Geoportal is a WebGIS portal built to give support to the conservation and the maintenance of the health and diversity of the natural resources within 
the park district. It is exclusively reserved for users of the NFD team and allows them to store, view and edit data, records, and field observations in a geographic web-based system.  

This documentation will give the NFD team members an overview of the portal and will guide them through its main functionalities.
